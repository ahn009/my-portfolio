### Blog SEO Best Practices
- **Word Count:** 1500-2500 words minimum
- **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters with primary keyword
- **Internal Links:** Link to 3-5 other relevant posts/pages
- **External Links:** Link to authoritative sources (React docs, Next.js docs)
- **Schema:** Add Article schema to all blog posts
- **Images:** Include 3-5 optimized images with alt text
